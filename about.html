About RETRO Foundation

The RETRO Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to helping needy children by collecting donations and providing essential support. Our mission is to make a positive impact in the lives of children who are facing adversity and lack basic necessities.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide access to education, healthcare, and basic amenities to underprivileged children worldwide. We believe that every child deserves a chance to thrive and reach their full potential, regardless of their background or circumstances.

Impact

Since our inception, we have reached thousands of children in need and have made a significant impact on their lives. Through our various programs and initiatives, we have helped improve access to education, healthcare, and nutrition, empowering children to build a brighter future for themselves and their communities.
